GAMING

Being a game programmer is anyone who works on developing a video game in any capacity.
There are many different areas of game development such as, graphics, physics, gameplay, audio, online, UI, etc.

$81K - $135K /yr

$101,752 /yr
Average UI programmer salary from ZipRecruiter (in the United States):
$121,666 /yr

$149,118 /yr
