Careers in

GAMING

Being a game programmer is anyone who works on developing a video game in any capacity.
There are many different areas of game development such as, graphics, physics, gameplay, audio, online, UI, etc.

Average game programmer salary from Glassdoor (in the United States):
$81K - $135K /yr
Average rendering engineer salary from ZipRecruiter (in the United States):
$101,752 /yr
Average UI programmer salary from ZipRecruiter (in the United States):
$121,666 /yr
Average Game Audio Designer salary from ZipRecruiter (in the United States):
$149,118 /yr

There are so many options to take within game development and almost all of them give great pay along with other benefits!
Game development is on the rise and will continue to be very profitable far into the foreseeable future.
