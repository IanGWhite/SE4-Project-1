Why CS?

Computer Science is a broad major that can tackle a lot of areas in the computer world. Choosing Computer Science will teach you all the skills you'll need for the real world. This page will give some of the benefits to chosing CS.

1. Diverse career opportunities
Because CS is so broad, there are lots of different jobs you can choose in several different areas.

2. Strong problem-solving skills
CS is all about problem solving. This major will grant you the skills needed to overcome any issue you may face in the real world. These skill also can transfer to any field in the real world such as finance or business.

3. Research skills
Computer science majors learn how to research, investigate and report. These important research skills are beneficial in a computer science work environment when completing tasks, like identifying security risks or assessing the technical needs of a business. Research can also be useful when finding solutions to problems quickly.

4. Networking opportunities
Computer science majors have many opportunities to network and find relationships that will be useful past school. Other students and professors will be able to help you find the resources you need to find a job. Some CS programs require getting an internship which will help a ton.

Source: Indeed - Reasons to be a Computer Science Major

Learn More!

Many people rely on computer scientists everyday, without giving it much thought until something doesn't work. From playing games on your phone to building the databases for hospitals, computer science is a career path that will always have jobs.

Computer science has many different types of lucrative jobs. In learning how to solve complex problems it can help you in your everyday life. The job will never grow stale, there will always be new things to explore. This can be seen with how quickly computers have gone from the size of entire buildings to far more sophiscated computers fitting in our pockets. If you decide you don't like one particular ascept of computer science you can choose a different area to work in. For me, I took gaming and animation classes in high school. I found out that it wasn't what I was wanting to do. Still, I am glad I took those classes because it helped me get better at learning new things.
